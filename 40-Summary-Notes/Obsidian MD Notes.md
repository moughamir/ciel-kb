# Obsidian MD Notes

This note summarizes information about Obsidian MD, a popular note-taking application.

## What is Obsidian MD?

-   A popular note-taking application.
-   Allows users to create, organize, and link notes using a markdown-based system.
-   Highly customizable.
-   Can be used for:
    -   Task management.
    -   Knowledge management.
    -   Creative writing.
-   Community-driven development with a wide range of third-party plugins and themes.

## How to Create a New Obsidian MD Vault

1.  Open Obsidian MD on your computer.
2.  Click on "Create a new vault" option.
3.  Choose the location where you want to store your new vault.
