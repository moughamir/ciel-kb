# Omnizya and Digital Flow Shop

This note summarizes information about the startup Omnizya and its project, the Digital Flow Shop (DFS).

## Omnizya

- **Founder:** Mohamed Moughamir (aka EisenUlfr)
- **Concept:** A personal brand started in 2009, now transitioning into a startup business.
- **Mission:** To provide innovative solutions and services to individuals and businesses in the digital age.
- **Services:** Web design and development, digital marketing, branding, e-commerce solutions, and software development.

## Mohamed Moughamir (EisenUlfr)

- **Born:** June 30th, 1990, in Settat, Morocco.
- **Education:** Studied Economics and Business Management at Hassan II University. Self-taught programmer.
- **Alias:** EisenUlfr

## Digital Flow Shop (DFS)

- **Concept:** An online digital agency that helps content creators define their goals and kickstart their careers.
- **Founded:** Q1 2023, as a project of Omnizya.
- **Core Team:**
    - Mohamed Moughamir (EisenUlfr)
    - Ora BoRass (Community Manager)
- **Community:** A WhatsApp community called "Digital Flow Creative Society".
- **Beneficiaries:** The project has helped several content creators, including:
    - **AbdelHadi Gomrani (Gymrani):** A fitness coach.
    - **AbdelGhani Gomrani (GomraniBirds & Nadra Mijharia):** A bird tamer and lifestyle blogger.
    - **Ahmed Mouzoun (Sultan Boomer):** A video gamer and crypto trader.
    - **Bilal Dimsi (DimsiArt):** An artist and bodybuilder.
