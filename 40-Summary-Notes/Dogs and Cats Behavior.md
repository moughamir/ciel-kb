# Dogs and Cats Behavior

This note summarizes the reasons why dogs and cats might not always get along.

## Reasons for Conflict

-   **Natural Differences in Behavior:** Dogs are often more social and pack-oriented, while cats are generally more independent.
-   **Communication Styles:** Their body language and ways of interacting can differ significantly, leading to misunderstandings, tension, or fear.

## Coexistence

-   Many dogs and cats can learn to coexist peacefully and even form close bonds.
-   Requires proper introductions, socialization, and a safe environment.
