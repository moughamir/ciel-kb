# Startup Roadmap Omnizya

This note summarizes the journey of Omnizya from a personal brand to a startup, founded by Mohamed Moughamir, and introduces the Digital Flow Shop (DFS) initiative.

## Omnizya: From Personal Brand to Startup

-   **Founder:** Mohamed Moughamir (aka EisenUlfr), born June 30th, 1990, in Settat City, Morocco.
-   **Background:** Studied Economics and Business Management, self-taught programming through web and hack forums.
-   **Evolution:** Started as a freelance web development and design service (Omnizya) in 2015, evolving into a comprehensive digital agency.
-   **Startup Vision (2020):** To develop and launch its own digital products and services, focusing on innovative and user-friendly digital solutions.
-   **Positioning:** Identified as a startup for better growth, flexibility, and collaboration opportunities, rather than a personal brand.

## Digital Flow Shop (DFS) Initiative

-   **Concept:** An online digital agency helping content creators find their way and define their goals before kickstarting a Digital Content Creator Career.
-   **Launch:** Started in Q1 2023 with 2 members, growing to 8 active members by the end of March.
-   **Community:** "Digital Flow Creative Society | مجتمع الإنسياب الرقمي للخـلاّقـيـن" on WhatsApp.
-   **Core Team:**

-   **Collaborator Members:** Ahmed Mouzoun, Bilal Dimsi, AbdelGhani Gomrani, AbdelHadi Gomrani, Soufiane Oqda, Ayoub Daim.

## Business Model Canvas for Digital Flow Shop

-   **Key Partners:** Content creators/influencers, freelance designers/developers, social media platforms, influencer marketing agencies.
-   **Key Activities:** Guidance/mentorship, brand identity/digital assets, social media/influencer marketing, web/graphic design, technical services, market research.
-   **Key Resources:** Core/collaborator team, social media platforms, online tools, network, customer data.
-   **Value Proposition:** Helps content creators define goals, provides mentorship, custom brand identity, technical support, marketing solutions, affordable pricing.
-   **Customer Segments:** Content creators/influencers, small businesses/startups, marketing/advertising agencies, freelancers.
-   **Channels:** Social media, digital advertising, word of mouth, SEO/content marketing.
-   **Customer Relationships:** Personalized mentorship, responsive customer service, regular communication, flexible pricing.
-   **Revenue Streams:** Consulting/mentorship fees, web/graphic design fees, technical services fees, social media/influencer marketing fees, advertising/promotional fees, commission from partners.

## Business Model Canvas for Omnizya

-   **Key Partners:** Collaborators/team members, platform providers, payment gateways.
-   **Key Activities:** Content creation/curation, guidance/support for digital content creators, developing/maintaining DFS platform.
-   **Key Resources:** Human resources, digital tools/technologies, intellectual property.
-   **Value Propositions:** Guidance/support for digital content creators, help define goals, brand identity development, community access.
-   **Customer Relationships:** Social media, email, messaging apps, personalized support.
-   **Channels:** Social media, email marketing, messaging apps.
-   **Customer Segments:** Digital content creators, aspiring digital content creators, small businesses.
-   **Cost Structure:** Human resources, digital tools/technologies, marketing/advertising.
-   **Revenue Streams:** Digital content creator services, advertising revenue from DFS, affiliate marketing.
