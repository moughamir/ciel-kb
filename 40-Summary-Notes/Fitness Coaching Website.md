# Fitness Coaching Website

This note summarizes the detailed specifications ("cahier des charges") for a fitness coaching website project, featuring video courses and a subscription model.

## 1. Objective

-   Provide online fitness courses accessible to all levels.
-   Offer personalized coaching programs.

## 2. Target Audience

-   Individuals of all ages and fitness levels seeking online fitness courses or personalized coaching.

## 3. Website Features

-   **Video Courses:** Online fitness video courses, organized by level and exercise type.
-   **Subscription:** Users can subscribe for access to video courses and personalized coaching.
-   **Personalized Coaching Programs:** Tailored programs created by qualified professionals.
-   **Workout Planning:** Users can plan workouts and track progress.
-   **Discussion Forum:** For user interaction and Q&A with coaches.
-   **Blog:** Articles and advice on nutrition, fitness, health, and well-being.
-   **Registration & Payment:** Easy and secure process with flexible payment options.
-   **Subscription Options:** Different tiers, competitive pricing, exclusive benefits.

## 4. Design & Ergonomics

-   Modern and attractive design.
-   Intuitive and easy-to-use navigation.
-   Compatible with common browsers and mobile devices.

## 5. Content

-   High quality and regularly updated.
-   Fitness videos by qualified professionals.
-   Blog content by domain experts.

## 6. Hosting & Maintenance

-   Reliable and secure server.
-   Regular technical maintenance for optimal performance and data security.

## 7. Marketing

-   Optimized for natural referencing (SEO) and social networks.
-   Online marketing campaigns to promote the site and attract users.

## 8. Budget & Deadlines

-   Clearly defined at the project start, based on objectives and features.

## 9. Security & Data Confidentiality

-   Effective security protocols for transmission and storage of sensitive data (payment info, personal data).

## 10. Technical Support & Customer Service

-   Efficient technical support for issues.
-   Customer service for user questions and problems.

## 11. Tracking & Analytics

-   Tools to measure marketing campaign effectiveness, monitor site performance, and identify improvements.

## 12. Social Media Integration

-   Allow users to share progress and promote the site on platforms like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, YouTube.

## 13. Updates & Evolution

-   Regular updates for new features and improved user experience.
-   User feedback integration for continuous improvement.

## 14. Multilingualism

-   Available in multiple languages to reach a wider audience.
