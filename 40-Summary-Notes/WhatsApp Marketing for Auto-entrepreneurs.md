# WhatsApp Marketing for Auto-entrepreneurs

This note summarizes strategies for auto-entrepreneur web developers to encourage interaction with their WhatsApp contacts on stories and status updates.

## Tips for Encouraging Interaction

1.  **Post Regularly:** Maintain consistent activity to stay top-of-mind.
2.  **Make Updates Interesting:** Share latest projects, showcase skills, or provide behind-the-scenes glimpses of work.
3.  **Use Visuals:** Incorporate photos or videos to make updates more eye-catching and stand out.
4.  **Ask Questions:** Engage contacts by asking questions related to your work or their needs.

## Short Audio Script (Max 15 sec)

"Hi there, this is [Your Name], a web developer and auto-entrepreneur. If you need a professional website or online presence, feel free to reach out to me. Let's build your online success together!"
