# Karba.link

Karba.link is a new service being developed by [[Omnizya and Digital Flow Shop|Omnizya]].

## Concept

A multi-tenant platform where users can create their own blog and share public profile links on social media. The goal of Karba.link is to facilitate the use of blogging platforms and ease the use of no-code tools.

## Technical Details

- **Multi-tenant architecture:** The platform is designed to support multiple users with their own separate blogs and profiles.
- **Prisma Schema:** The project plans to use Prisma with a PostgreSQL database (likely on Supabase) with multiple schemas for different models (Account, Public, Billings, Storage).

## Blog Content (open.karba.link)

-   **Title:** Open Source Insights: Exploring the Latest Technologies
-   **Description:** A blog providing in-depth analysis and commentary on open-source technologies, covering programming languages, frameworks, and tools, from beginner guides to advanced tutorials.

## Privacy Policy

-   **User Rights:** Users can CRUD their personal data via Account settings.
-   **Cookies & Tracking:** Uses cookies and other tracking technologies (Google Analytics, Meta Pixels) to improve user experience and optimize service. Users can manage cookie preferences in browser settings.
-   **Account Security:** Users must not share account access. Karba Link is not responsible for misuse.
